About Us

Built on Integrity,
Driven by Service

The Tanguay Agency was founded by Mike Tanguay, a Managing General Agent with Globe Life's American Income Division. Based in Southgate, Michigan, we serve families throughout the state with competitively priced life, accident, and supplemental health insurance.

Our mission is simple — protect the people who protect others. We proudly serve members of labor unions, credit unions, and associations alongside their families, meeting them where they're most comfortable.
