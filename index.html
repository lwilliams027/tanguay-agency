About Us

Built on Belief.
Driven by Purpose.

The Tanguay Agency was built on a simple belief: every family deserves protection, and every hardworking person deserves the opportunity to build something meaningful. Founded by Master General Agent Mike Tanguay of The Schrieber Organization, part of Globe Life's American Income Division, our agency is headquartered in Southgate, Michigan and proudly serves families throughout Michigan, Florida, Texas, Ohio, Oklahoma, and Missouri.

Our mission is simple: protect the people who protect others. For decades, American Income Life has served members of labor unions, credit unions, and professional associations — teachers, tradespeople, first responders, healthcare workers, and countless others who work hard to support their families. Our role is to ensure their families are financially protected when the unexpected happens.

Meet Tandem

The purpose-built power dialing system that sits right on top of your Impact Portal. One tap and it starts burning through your lead list — dialing, detecting voicemails, logging results, and moving to the next lead without you lifting a finger. When a real person picks up, it unmutes your mic, plays a chime in your ear, and you're live.

✓
Power Dialing — Automatically calls through your lead list. No tab switching, no copy-pasting numbers, no clicking through screens to log a result.
✓
Smart AMD — Answering machine detection identifies humans vs voicemails in under 3 seconds. Voicemail? Logged and moving on. Human? You're connected.
✓
Schedule Sync — Tap the sync icon and Tandem pulls your appointments from Impact. Leads with booked appointments get skipped automatically.
✓
Double-Tap Mode — Calls the lead twice before moving on, roughly doubling your contact rate.

How It Works

From Button Press
to Ringing Phone

Tandem scrapes your lead info directly from Impact — name, phone number, lead position — and places the call through Plivo's voice network using your own dedicated local number.

01
Scrape & Dial
The extension reads your lead list directly from Impact Portal and starts dialing. No exports, no imports, no manual entry.
02
Detect & Route
Machine detection runs in the background. Voicemail? Tandem hangs up, logs "No Answer," and moves to the next lead. Human? You're connected instantly.
03
Talk & Close
When a human picks up, your mic unmutes and a chime plays in your ear. You're live on the call — ready to sell, book, or follow up.
