About Us

Built on Belief.
Driven by Purpose.

The Tanguay Agency was built on a simple belief: every family deserves protection, and every hardworking person deserves the opportunity to build something meaningful. Founded by Master General Agent Mike Tanguay of The Schrieber Organization, part of Globe Life's American Income Division, our agency is headquartered in Southgate, Michigan and proudly serves families throughout Michigan, Florida, Texas, Ohio, Oklahoma, and Missouri.

Our mission is simple: protect the people who protect others. For decades, American Income Life has served members of labor unions, credit unions, and professional associations — teachers, tradespeople, first responders, healthcare workers, and countless others who work hard to support their families. Our role is to ensure their families are financially protected when the unexpected happens.
